About Us


Incorporated in 1993, DPI Industries Sdn Bhd was established in Malaysia with the aim of manufacturing world-class electrical products for quality-conscious consumers. Since then, with a paid up capital of RM1 million, we have steadily grown and have achieved significant international market penetration in countries such as USA, Australia, New Zealand, China, Hong Kong, Vietnam, Singapore, Indonesia, Thailand and Cambodia.

Here at DPI Industries, we emphasize product quality and customer service as we believe that these qualities are the backbone required to establish lasting relationships with our customers.
