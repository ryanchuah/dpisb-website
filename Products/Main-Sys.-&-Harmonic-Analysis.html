Main Sys. & Harmonic Analysis

Many commercial, industrial and residential installations have problems with the quality and efficient utilization of their power supply including:

  • Maximum demand differentiation
  • Low power factor
  • High harmonic contents
  • Low dielectric losses (<0.1W/kVAr)

Main System Analysis

For such installations, DPI can provide a complete mains system study to identify the nature and magnitude of the problems. This same exercise can also be used to provide an energy audit if so required.
Our trained site personnel and inhouse analysis engineers will provide a comprehensive report complete with recommendations for preventive and corrective measures where applicable.

PF & Harmonic Analysis

In line with our provision of the total solution for power factor correction, DPI will also conduct site analysis specifically to obtain data for the proper design of power factor correction equipment for the installation.

These include:

  • Determination of the reactive power requirement
  • Recommendation on the type of capacitors to use
  • Analysis of the harmonic content in the mains
  • Recommendation on type of harmonic filters to use
  • Recommendation on the type of switchgear to use
  • Assistance in the design of capacitor bank
